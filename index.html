The Voter Journey

Your interactive guide to the election process, timelines, and steps.

1

Preparation (Registration)

Before you can vote, you must be registered. Deadlines vary by state, so check early!

  • Registration Deadline: Often 15-30 days before the election.
  • Requirements: US Citizen, 18+ years old, resident of your state.
2

Education (Research)

Learn about who and what is on your ballot. An informed voter is a powerful voter.

  • Primaries vs General Elections.
  • Research Ballot Measures and local candidates.
3

Action (Casting the Ballot)

It's time to make your voice heard. Choose the method that works best for you.

  • Early Voting
  • Mail-in Ballot
  • Election Day: Polling places are usually open from 7 AM to 8 PM.
4

Conclusion (The Count)

The polls have closed. Now the meticulous process of counting begins.

  • Initial results are projections. Official certification takes weeks.
  • Electoral College convenes in December.
